LIGHT SOUP

Description

Ghanaian light soup is a spicy soup with chicken, beef or goat and it's usually paired with fufu or rice.

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • Raw tomato
  • Onions
  • Habanero
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken/Beef/Goat meat
  • Steps
    1. Steam your proteins for 20 minutes
    2. Add tomato paste to the proteins
    3. Blend your vegetables and add to your proteins
    4. Add 1 cup of water
    5. Cook for 45 minutes on medium heat
    Voila!!! you have a spicy ghanaian light soup
