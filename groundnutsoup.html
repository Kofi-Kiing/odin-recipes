GROUNDNUT SOUP

Description

Ghanaian groundnut soup is a soup made of peanut butter and some protein of choice mainly chicken, beef, goat meat, or combination of all.

Usually paired with fufu, banku or rice ball.

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • Raw tomato
  • Onions
  • Habanero
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Peanut butter
  • Chicken/Beef/Goat meat
  • Steps
    1. Mix tomato paste, peanut butter, water and cook until the mixture begins to solidify
    2. Steam your Proteins
    3. Blend your vegetables and add to your proteins
    4. Cook for 15 minutes to 20 minutes
    5. Add peanut butter mixture to the proteins
    6. Add half a cup of water
    7. Cook extra 25 minutes
    Voila!!! you have a rich groundnut soup
