Description

Ghanaian jollof is a spicy rice that’s cooked in a rich tomato and pepper sauce with a touch of natural spices, it has a nice flavor.

Usually paired with plantains and some fried or grilled chicken, it’s a whole vibe on a plate.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Stew Base
    • Tomato paste
    • Raw tomato
    • Onions
    • Habanero
    • Bell Pepper
    • Natural spices
  • Chicken breast/thigh/breast
  • Ripe plantain
  • Steps
    1. Cook your tomato paste for minutes to 10 minutes
    2. Bake your vegetables for 10 minutes, blend and add to your tomato paste
    3. Add your natural spices and cook the stew on medium heat for 25 minutes
    4. Add your washed rice to the stew
    5. Add enough water or meat stock to the rice and stew
    6. Cover with aluminium foil
    7. Set to medium heat and allow to cook for 40 minutes
    8. Add your vegetables and some buteer and cook for extra 10 minutes.
    Voila!!! you have your authentic ghanaian jollof.
